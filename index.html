About Us

Founded in 1993, Independent University,
Bangladesh is one of the oldest private universities in Bangladesh where academic excellence is a tradition, teaching a passion and lifelong learning a habit.
IUB currently has more than 7,048 undergraduate and graduate students and over 10,455 alumni.
The student population is projected to grow at 10% annually.
The students of IUB experience an exciting academic life with copious opportunity to explore and nurture their innate talent.
